Action Animes



  1. Naruto & Naruto shippuden


    Naruto is one of the most iconic anime and manga series, created by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and earning the title of Hokage, the leader of his village. The series, known for its emotional storytelling, epic battles, and themes of perseverance and friendship, is divided into two parts:
    Naruto (Original Series): Covers Naruto's early years and training as a young ninja.
    Naruto: Shippuden: Focuses on Naruto's growth as a teenager and his journey to protect his friends and village.


  2. Bleach

    Bleach is a popular action-fantasy anime and manga series created by Tite Kubo. Known for its stylish characters, epic battles, and intricate lore, Bleach revolves around the world of Soul Reapers (Shinigami), spirits, and Hollows. The series originally aired from 2004 to 2012 and later returned in 2022 with the Thousand-Year Blood War arc, adapting the manga's final storyline.
