Romance Animes


  1. Your Name


    Your Name is a romantic fantasy drama about two teenagers, Mitsuha Miyamizu and Taki Tachibana, who mysteriously begin switching bodies. Mitsuha, a girl from a rural town, dreams of escaping her small-town life, while Taki, a boy from Tokyo, juggles school and a part-time job. Their connection unfolds in unexpected ways as they experience each other's lives, leading to a deeper bond.


  2. Your Lie in April.


    Your Lie in April (Shigatsu wa Kimi no Uso) is a heart-wrenching anime series based on the manga by Naoshi Arakawa. It explores themes of love, loss, and the healing power of music. The story centers on Kousei Arima, a young pianist who loses his ability to hear the sound of his piano after his mother's death, and Kauri Miyazono, a spirited violinist who helps him rediscover his love for music.
